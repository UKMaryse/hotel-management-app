WEDDINGS & HONEYMOONS

WEDDINGS IN KIGALI

Here at Kigali Serena Hotel in Rwanda, we treasure each wedding we host as if it were our own and provide gracious hospitality and 5-star settings to ensure an unforgettable celebration of family and friends. From the earliest planning stages until the final guests have departed, we are committed to designing fully customized gatherings that exceed all expectations.

We offer intimate venues for small family affairs and larger spaces that can accommodate grand gala receptions of up to 800 guests. With its own gracious entrance, marble lobby and selection of function rooms, our stand-alone events center also features a full stage, outdoor terraces, and landscaped grounds and sparkling fountains to create a unique and unforgettable backdrop for your wedding day.

HONEYMOONS

Your wedding has come and gone and you are on your way to creating your life together. But first, why not relax, de-stress from wedding planning and reflect on your special day? Just the two of you. A honeymoon at the Kigali Serena Hotel has it all: unrivalled elegance and comfort, a delightful pool deck and poolside café, all the services of the Maisha Health Club, and the option of dinner for two in our beautifully presented Milima Restaurant.

Additional romantic possibilities include:

  • Candlelit dinner for two on your private balcony
  • Surprise dinner or cocktails for two on a pool terrace
  • Champagne sundowners on the pool deck
  • ‘His and Hers’ massage in your room
  • Extension weekends to our exclusive Natia Hotel which lies on the shores of Africa’s sixth largest inland lake and within easy reach of the Volcanoes National Park, home to Rwanda’s world-famous mountain gorillas.
